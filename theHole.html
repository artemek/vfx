THE HOLE

The first course project of the Team Green from the 9th class of the VFX Artist course.

Based on Ken Lee's Shelter:DAWN concept art project

Personal contribution

The animations (camera, shuttle movement), splitting render into AOVs, and rendering settings were done by me. Also participated in lightning.

White color – modeling and shading.

Blue color – shading only.

Black color – did not participate.

Procedural Modeling

For the project, I created a number of procedural assets to introduce variety into repetitive elements and enable quick adjustments to the final appearance of the assets when changes were needed

Shading

The shading, like most of the modeling, was done using a procedural approach. This made it easy to quickly adjust the final look of an asset and reuse the created shaders across different geometry.

Vellum and Particle simulation

To bring more life and realism to the shot, simulations of cloth and particles were created. These dynamic elements added natural motion and helped enhance the overall visual storytelling, making the scene feel more immersive and detailed

Sound design

A sound design has been developed to add more integrity to the project. Sounds from libraries were used with additions of various post effects and mixing
